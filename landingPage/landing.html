About Your Note

As a passionate learner struggling with complex study materials, I realized students need a more intuitive way to understand and retain information. Your Note was born from the personal challenge of transforming complex academic concepts into digestible, personalized learning experiences.

This project is my Portfolio Project for ALX, representing a solution to personalized learning challenges in education.
